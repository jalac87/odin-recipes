Pepperoni Pizza Recipe

Description

There are few joys in life greater than biting into a slice of hot, cheesy, zesty pizza pie. When I was a lad, I used to go play hopscotch over in Hell's Kitchen, and ol' Papa Sal would always offer a slice on my way through. Oh, the excitement with every bite!

This recipe is an attempt to reconnect with those childhood feelings. To really feel that joy and happiness on your tongue, just like in the old days. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 16 oz pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 20 slices of pepperoni
  • 12 oz mozzarella cheese, grated
  • 1 tsp fresh oregano
  • Flour for rolling dough

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 500F
  2. Make the sauce
  3. Roll out the dough
  4. Add the toppings
  5. Cook the pizza
  6. Slice and serve
