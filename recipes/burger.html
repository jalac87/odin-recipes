Burger Recipe

Description

Burgers are made from cow meat.

Moo. Put cheese on a burger and what do you get?

Why, a cheeseburger, of course!

Ingredients

  • Ground chuck
  • Panko bread crumbs
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Add beef, egg, panko, milk, sauce, and spices to mixing bowl
  2. Form disks and divide mixture into 6 patties
  3. Press patties to desired thickness
  4. Preheat grill to 350-400F
  5. Fry patties for 2-4 minutes per side, depending on thickness
  6. Serve on a bun with toppings of your choice
