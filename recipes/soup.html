Chicken Noodle Soup Recipe

Description

It's soup.

It is good.

Yum.

YUMM!!!!!!!!!

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots
  • 2 celery ribs
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 8 cups chicken stock
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2.5 pounds chicken breasts
  • 2.5 cups wide egg noodles
  • 2 tbsp parsley leaves
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Steps

  1. Melt butter
  2. Stir in stock
  3. Stir in chicken and pasta
  4. Remove from heat and stir in herbs
  5. Serve immediately
